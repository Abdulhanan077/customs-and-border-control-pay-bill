OFFICIAL NOTICE OF MERCHANDISE DETENTION

This notification is issued pursuant to 19 U.S.C. Section 1499 to advise you that U.S. Customs and Border Protection (CBP) has detained the imported merchandise described above for examination and regulatory verification. The shipment has been flagged pending the submission of requisite customs entry documentation, identity validation, and surety bond verification.

Under federal import regulations, all incoming international cargo must be covered by a valid Customs Bond (19 CFR Part 113) or hold a formally approved clearance waiver. The cargo is currently held at our secure terminal facility. Release for final delivery and transfer of custody to the domestic carrier is conditioned upon the submission of a valid bond and the settlement of all outstanding statutory customs duties, taxes, and associated administrative fees.
